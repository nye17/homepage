Curriculum vitae


I will be an assistant professor at Shanghai Jiao Tong University , starting Sep 2017. Prior to that, I was an undergraduate at Nanjing University, a grduate student at Ohio State Astronomy, a postdoc at the McWilliams Center for Cosmology of Carnegie Mellon and a long term CCAPP fellow at the Center for Cosmology and Astro-Particle Physics.


Please check out my CV if you want to know more. Outside of Astronomy, I enjoy reading antique books, going to concerts, and writing tiny apps.
