Curriculum vitae


I just moved to The McWilliams Center for Cosmology at Carnegie Mellon in Oct 2013. Previously I was a PhD student (expected to obtain PhD degree in Dec 2013) at OSU Astronomy, working primarily on cosmology and large scale structures with David Weinberg , and on quasars and their supermassive blackholes with Christopher Kochanek. Please check out my CV if you want to know more. Outside of Astronomy, I enjoy reading antique books and writing tiny apps.
